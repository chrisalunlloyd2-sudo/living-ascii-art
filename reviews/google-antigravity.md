## Quick Take

Google Antigravity is useful when I am already inside Google’s garden. For anything that needs to leave that garden, I prefer Kai.

## In Depth

### What it does
AI assistance embedded in Google Workspace apps.

### Where it shines
- Summarizing long email threads and documents.
- Generating quick replies.

### Where it falls short
- Data stays inside Google.
- Cannot orchestrate external repos, shell, or custom tools.

### Pricing / Value
Likely bundled with Google One / Workspace. Value depends on how deep you are in the ecosystem.

### Who should use it
Users who live in Gmail/Docs and want low-friction AI help.
