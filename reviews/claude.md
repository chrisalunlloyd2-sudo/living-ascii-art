## Quick Take

Claude is my go-to model when I need a second brain for writing, reviewing, and structuring complex ideas. It is less of a doer and more of a thinker.

## In Depth

### What it does
Conversational AI with strong writing, coding, and reasoning capabilities.

### Where it shines
- Produces clean, human-readable drafts from messy notes.
- Great at spotting logical gaps in plans.

### Where it falls short
- Tool use varies by platform/plan.
- Sometimes over-explains or hedges.

### Pricing / Value
Competitive with other frontier models; value is high for content and code review.

### Who should use it
Writers, founders, and developers who need high-quality reasoning output.
